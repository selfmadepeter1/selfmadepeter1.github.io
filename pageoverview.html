Project Overview

1. Project Name

Fit and Flow: A Wellness Resource for Women at UNC Charlotte

2. Project Overview

  • Purpose: To promote mental and physical wellness among women at UNC Charlotte by providing accessible resources for fitness, nutrition, and mindfulness.
  • Intended Users: Female students at UNC Charlotte interested in health, wellness, and community support.
  • Overview of Content: This site will include fitness videos, yoga tutorials, healthy recipes, upcoming events, testimonials, and an interactive contact form to join events or ask questions.

6. Page Design

Home (index.html)

  • Purpose: To welcome users and provide an overview of the site.
  • Audience: All users.
  • Content: Mission statement, featured fitness tips, quick links to resources.
  • Data Entry: No
  • Validations: N/A
  • Buttons/Links: Navigation bar, feature buttons
  • Actions: Navigate to other pages

About Us

  • Purpose: To explain who we are, our goals, and our story.
  • Audience: New users, potential collaborators
  • Content: Text, images of members, values
  • Data Entry: No
  • Buttons/Links: Internal links, collapsible FAQ using jQuery UI
  • Actions: View bio info, read mission

Fitness & Wellness

  • Purpose: To provide fitness videos, yoga routines, and motivation.
  • Audience: Students interested in workouts
  • Content: Video embeds, workout plans
  • Data Entry: No
  • Buttons/Links: Filters, expand video lightbox
  • Actions: Watch videos and read content

Nutrition

  • Purpose: To offer easy, student-friendly recipes and nutrition advice.
  • Audience: Students who cook or meal prep
  • Content: Recipe cards, grocery lists, downloadable PDFs
  • Data Entry: No
  • Buttons/Links: Dropdowns, download links
  • Actions: View, download or print recipes

Contact / Join Us

  • Purpose: To allow users to reach out, RSVP, or join the community
  • Audience: Potential members, collaborators
  • Content: Form with name, email, interest area
  • Data Entry: Yes
  • Validations: Required fields, valid email
  • Buttons/Links: Submit button
  • Actions: Submit info with JavaScript, confirm submission

7. Dynamic Functionality

  • Form Validation (Contact Page): Ensures fields like name, email are filled and properly formatted
  • Interactive Gallery (Fitness Page): Clickable images open in lightbox using jQuery plugin
  • AJAX Tips Section (Home): Loads random fitness/mindfulness tip from a local JSON file without refreshing
  • jQuery UI Accordion (About Page): Expanding sections with more info
