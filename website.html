Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple's website is a strong example of excellent design. It's visually appealing, easy to navigate, and uses white space effectively.

  • Contrast: High contrast between text and background enhances readability.
  • Repetition: Consistent design elements like fonts, buttons, and layout styles unify the site.
  • Alignment: Precise grid alignment creates a clean and organized structure.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped together logically, improving comprehension.

Arngren.net

This site is often used as an example of poor web design due to its cluttered layout and bad design.

  • Contrast: Poor contrast makes text hard to read against the background.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent styles and lack of cohesion throughout the pages.
  • Alignment: Elements appear scattered with no alignment, causing confusion.
  • Proximity: Unrelated items are bunched together, making it difficult to scan content efficiently.
